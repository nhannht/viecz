# [8] Vietnam's Gig Economy for Foreign Firms

**Source:** Vietnam Briefing
**URL:** https://www.vietnam-briefing.com/news/vietnams-gig-economy-for-foreign-firms.html/
**Published:** October 2023

## Worker Preferences

- Only **40%** of knowledge workers desire full-time traditional office work
- **56%** prefer hybrid work arrangements
- **4%** have left traditional jobs for freelance gig work

## Major Platforms by Sector

| Sector | Platforms |
|---|---|
| Ride-hailing & Delivery | Grab, Gojek, Be |
| Online Education | Topica Edtech, Prep.vn, Hocmai.vn, ELSA |
| E-commerce | Shopee, Lazada, TikTok Shop, TIKI, Sendo |
| Freelancing | Freelancer.com, Upwork, Fiverr, Workana |

## Online Education Market

- Revenue projected: **US$328.20 million** (2023)
- Expected CAGR 2023–2027: **10%**
- Projected 2027: **US$487.60 million**

## Key Trends

- Gig economy expanded significantly during COVID-19
- Workers value flexibility and immediate compensation
- "Quickly find work in a matter of seconds, complete a task, receive immediate compensation"
