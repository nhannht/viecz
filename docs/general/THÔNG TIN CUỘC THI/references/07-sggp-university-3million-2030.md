# [7] Vietnam's University Student Count to Surpass 3 Million by 2030

**Source:** Sài Gòn Giải Phóng (English)
**URL:** https://en.sggp.org.vn/vietnams-university-student-count-to-surpass-3-million-by-2030-post116038.html
**Published:** March 2025

## Primary Goal

Vietnam aims to expand university student population to **exceed 3 million by 2030**, up from current **2.1 million**.

## Key Enrollment Targets

- **33%** of individuals aged 18–22 enrolled in university
- No province below **15%** enrollment rate
- **260** undergraduate and **23** postgraduate students per 10,000 people (currently 230/10,000)

## Training Distribution Goals

| Category | Target |
|---|---|
| STEM programs | 35% of total training |
| Master's level | 7.2% of training capacity |
| Doctoral level | 0.8% of training capacity |
| Pedagogy | 1% of training capacity |
| STEM students by 2030 | ~1 million |

## Institutional Structure

- Public universities: ~70% of national training capacity
- Private and non-profit: ~30% of national training capacity
- Teacher training universities: consolidated to 48–50 institutions
