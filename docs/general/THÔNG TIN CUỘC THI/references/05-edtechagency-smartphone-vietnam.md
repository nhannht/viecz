# [5] Vietnam Smartphone Penetration

**Source:** Edtech Agency
**URL:** https://edtechagency.net/vietnam-aiming-for-100-smartphone-used-by-the-end-of-2024/
**Published:** March 2024

## Key Statistics

- **Smartphone penetration: >84%** of Vietnam's population
- Government target: **100% smartphone adoption** by end of 2024

## Supporting Infrastructure

- 4G coverage: **99.8%** (vs 99.4% in high-income nations)
- Fiber optic household coverage: ~80% (vs 60% global average)
- Data costs: approximately **half the worldwide average**

## Digital Readiness

- Vietnam ranks **9th globally** and **2nd within ASEAN** for IPv6 adoption
- Strong positioning for IoT expansion and digital economy growth
