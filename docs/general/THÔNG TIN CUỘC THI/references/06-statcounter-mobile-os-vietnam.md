# [6] Mobile OS Market Share in Vietnam

**Source:** Statista / StatCounter
**URL:** https://www.statista.com/statistics/928956/vietnam-mobile-os-share/
**Note:** Exact percentages behind paywall. Approximate figures from KNS-A-1 and other sources.

## Key Data

- **Android:** ~65.7% market share (dominant)
- **iOS:** ~33.7% market share (second)
- Samsung remaining share

## Context

- Data spans 2009–2025, sourced from StatCounter (15+ billion page views/month)
- Vietnam has one of Asia's highest smartphone penetration rates
- Apple and Samsung compete fiercely, collectively holding ~2/3 of total mobile phone market share
- Budget phones popular: Xiaomi, Samsung A-series, Oppo
