# [2] Zalo User Statistics and Market Position

**Source:** VietnamNet
**URL:** https://vietnamnet.vn/en/zalo-s-number-of-users-hits-78-3-million-putting-telcos-at-pipeline-trap-2436470.html
**Published:** August 2025

## User Base

- **78.3 million** monthly active users as of mid-2025
- Surpasses Viettel (>50% of Vietnam's mobile market share)

## Messaging Volume

- **2 billion messages** sent daily
- Nearly 1 million users utilize voice-to-text messaging daily
- 5 million users engage with voice-to-text monthly

## AI Adoption

- 20 million monthly users engage with Zalo's AI features
- Voice-to-text is a key AI feature integrated into chat windows

## Market Dominance

"Zalo continues to grow, becoming Vietnam's leading messaging app" — top position in the OTT messaging sector.

## Impact on Traditional Services

Growth has significantly eroded demand for SMS and ringtone services. Users are shifting from operator alternatives to Zalo's own ringtone/ringback tone services.
