# [9] TaskRabbit Business and Revenue Model

**Source:** InfoStride
**URL:** https://infostride.com/taskrabbit-business-model/
**Published:** May 2025

## Company Overview

- **Founded:** 2008 by Leah Busque (originally "RunMyErrand")
- **Acquired:** 2017 by IKEA
- **HQ:** San Francisco, California
- **Operating:** 70+ metropolitan areas across 6 countries (USA, UK, Canada, Germany, France, Spain)
- **Total Funding:** $37.7 million across 7 rounds

## Key Metrics

- **4+ million** tasks completed
- **1.5+ million** users served
- Tens of thousands of active Taskers
- **45+ service categories**
- Insurance coverage up to **$1,000,000** per task

## Revenue Streams

1. **Commission Fee (15%):** Percentage of overall price clients pay
2. **Trust & Support Fee (7.5%):** Added to each invoice for safety, support, operations
3. **Registration Fee ($25):** Non-refundable fee from new Tasker sign-ups

## Business Model

Two-sided peer-to-peer marketplace:
- **TaskPosters** (service seekers): People needing help with household tasks, errands, repairs
- **Taskers** (service providers): Independent contractors offering skills

Platform enables: post tasks → match with vetted providers → communicate via app → process payments digitally
