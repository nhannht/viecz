# [10] TaskRabbit Business Model and Revenue

**Source:** Appscrip
**URL:** https://appscrip.com/blog/taskrabbit-business-model/
**Published:** December 2024

## Revenue Growth

| Year | Revenue |
|------|---------|
| 2020 | $45.3 million |
| 2021 | $63.1 million |
| 2022 | $94.3 million |
| 2023 | $117.5 million |

## Revenue Streams

1. **Service fees:** Percentage of transaction amounts
2. **Trust & support fee:** 7.5% on each completed task
3. **Commission:** 15% cut off what a Tasker makes
4. **Peak pricing:** Surge pricing during high demand
5. **Advertising fees:** Charges to Taskers seeking visibility
6. **Subscription:** TaskRabbit for Business (corporate clients)

## Key Milestones

- **2008:** Founded in Boston (originally RunMyErrand)
- **2013:** Mobile app launched
- **2017:** Acquired by IKEA

## Features

- Rigorous vetting process including background checks
- AI-powered matching algorithms
- Real-time chat and GPS tracking
- Customer rating/review systems
